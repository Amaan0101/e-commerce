                Overview

                Seamless Multitasking

                The high-speed 3.4GHz Intel Core i5-1335U 13th Gen processor in the HP Pavilion 15 Windows Laptop promises a fast and smooth performance. Furthermore, with 16GB DDR4 RAM, this laptop enables multitasking, allowing you to seamlessly toggle between various heavy applications and software.


                Big and Bright Display

                Enjoy a visual feast on the 15.6-inch IPS screen of this laptop, which showcases true-to-life colours and dynamic visuals for an unparalleled entertainment experience. Moreover, with its 60Hz refresh rate, this laptop eliminates motion blur during fast-paced scenes.


                Dual Speakers

                Integrated with dual speakers, this 15.6-inch laptop creates an engaging and dynamic audio atmosphere. So, you can feel the thrill of your favourite adventure movie scenes or games as if you're right in the heart of the action.


                Crystal-Clear Audio

                Experience pristine audio quality as AI noise removal effectively blocks unwanted noise, while ensuring your privacy remains under your control with the convenient mute mic hotkey.


                Intel Iris Xe Graphics Card

                Whether you're a gamer or a content creator, the Intel Iris Xe graphics card provides the performance needed for demanding tasks.


                1TB M.2 PCIe NVMe SSD

                Built with a generous 1TB M.2 PCIe NVMe SSD, this laptop ensures rapid data access, minimises loading times, and promises fast boot-ups, thereby contributing to an enhanced level of system responsiveness.


                720p HD Webcam and Integrated Dual Array Digital Microphones

                Equipped with a high-resolution 720p HD webcam, this laptop ensures that your video conferencing experience is enhanced with crystal-clear visuals. Moreover, its temporal noise reduction and integrated dual array digital microphones ensure precise audio capture so that your voice is clear and audible in virtual meetings.


                Windows 11 Home Operating System

                Owing to its Windows 11 Home operating system, this laptop ensures a user-friendly interface, fast performance, and broad application support for a versatile computing environment.


                Large Battery Capacity

                With a formidable 41Wh lithium-ion, this laptop ensures a prolonged battery life, allowing you to enjoy uninterrupted productivity and entertainment for extended periods.


                Seamless Connectivity Options

                Equipped with a USB 3.2 Type-C port, two USB 3.2 ports, an HDMI 2.1 port, and a 3.5mm AUX port, this HP laptop provides you with the freedom to connect to peripherals without any hassle. Additionally, stay connected on the go with this laptop's IEEE 802.11a/b/g/n/ac/ax Wi-Fi specification, which ensures swift and reliable internet access.
